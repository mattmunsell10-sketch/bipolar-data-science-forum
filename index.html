Our Mission

Why this space exists

Most research on bipolar disorder is written about us, not for us or by us. Academic papers sit behind paywalls. Clinical language creates distance. And the lived knowledge of millions of people navigating this disorder daily is rarely treated as expertise.

The Bipolar Data Science Forum adds something new: a place where people with bipolar disorder can also be the builders — applying data science to their own minds, sharing code and datasets, and producing analyses that academic researchers can't, because they don't live it.

We hold space for complexity. Science and suffering coexist here.

🔍

Science first

Only peer-reviewed sources in the library. Primary literature, not pop summaries.

📊

Data by us

Community-built projects. People with BD as researchers, not just research subjects.

🔓

Always free

No paywalls, no ads. All research and projects are permanently open.

⚖️

Honest complexity

We don't sanitize the difficulty. Hope and hardship are both welcome here.
